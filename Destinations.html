Mirissa

A relaxed coastal town known for whale watching, surfing, and stunning sunsets.

  • Whale and Dolphin watching
  • Coconut Tree Hill Viewpoint
  • Secret Beach and quiet coves
  • Best time: April
  • Travel time: 2–3 hours from Colombo

Negombo

Negombo is a lively coastal city with golden beaches, Dutch colonial architecture, and a busy fishing harbor.

  • Dutch Canal & colonial buildings
  • Historic churches & temples
  • Fresh seafood markets
  • Best time: December to April
  • Travel time: 30 minutes from Colombo

Nearby Attractions: Muthurajawela Marsh, Colombo City

  • World-class surfing
  • Lagoon safaris
  • Beachside nightlife

  • Travel time: 7-8 hours from Colombo

Trincomalee

Coastal city with pristine beaches, rich marine life, and historic temples. Ideal for diving and whale watching.

  • Nilaveli & Uppuveli Beaches
  • Fort Frederick
  • Pigeon Island Marine Park
  • Best time: April to October
  • Travel time: 6 hours from Colombo

Nearby Attractions: Hot Springs of Kanniya, Marble Beach

Sigiriya

Sigiriya, or the 'Lion Rock', is an ancient fortress and palace built atop a massive rock. A UNESCO World Heritage site.

  • 5th-century rock fortress and palace ruins
  • Iconic frescoes of the ‘Heavenly Maidens’
  • Ancient Mirror Wall with historic graffiti
  • Best time: March to May, September to November
  • Travel time: 4–5 hours from Colombo

Nearby Attractions: Minneriya National Park, Pidurangala Rock

Inquire Now

Dambulla

A historic town home to Sri Lanka’s largest cave temple complex. A UNESCO spiritual landmark.

  • Five caves with 150+ Buddha statues
  • Colorful cave paintings over 2,000 years old
  • Golden Temple with a giant Buddha statue
  • Best time: Year-round
  • Travel time: 4 hours from Colombo

Nearby Attractions: Sigiriya Rock Fortress, Pidurangala Rock

Inquire Now

Jaffna

Cultural heart of Sri Lanka’s Tamil heritage, known for historic temples, colonial sites, and vibrant markets.

  • Nallur Kandaswamy Temple
  • Tamil cuisine specialties
  • Point Pedro Lighthouse
  • Best time: May to September
  • Travel time: 8 hours from Colombo

Nearby Attractions: Delft Island, Nagadipa Buddhist Temple
